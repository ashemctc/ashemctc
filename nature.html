Nature

Here are some things in the natural world that interest me!

I enjoy and appreciate aspects of nature like...

  • Great Barrier Reef

    • The Great Barrier Reef is the largest coral reef system that stretches over 1,400 miles off the coast of Ausralia.

    The reef itself is so large in size it can be seen from outer space, and considered the largest single structure made by living organisms.

  • Red Panda's

    I chose Red Pands as something in nature I like simply because to me, they're the closet thing we have to 'Pokemon' being as cute as they are.

    Red Panda's are native to the Himalayas and southwestern China and currently listed as Endangered with a wild population of (estimated) 10,000 or less. Due to Habitat loss poaching and other factors.

    Curious little creatures the Red Panda is a territorial animal and mostly solitary expect during mating season. They are fairly quiet expect for some twittering, tweeting and other forms of whistling sounds for comummincation.

  • Aurora Borealis

    Finally just due to its beauty I chose to showcase the Aurora Borealis as something in nature I think is interesting.

    It is a natural light display in the Earth's sky and is predominantly seen in the high-lattitude regions such as the Artic and the Antarctic.
